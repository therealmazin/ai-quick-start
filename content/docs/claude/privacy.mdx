# Protect Your Privacy in Claude

By default, Claude may use your conversations to improve its AI models. If you want to keep your chats private, you should turn this off.

<Callout type="warn">
We recommend turning off data training, especially if you discuss sensitive or work-related topics.
</Callout>

## How to Turn Off Data Training

<Steps>
<Step>
**Open Your Profile**

Click your profile picture or icon (usually in the bottom-left or top-right corner).
</Step>
<Step>
**Go to Settings**

Click on **Settings** from the menu.
</Step>
<Step>
**Find Privacy Settings**

Click on **Privacy**, then **Privacy Settings**.
</Step>
<Step>
**Turn Off "Help improve Claude"**

Find the toggle for **"Help improve Claude"** (or similar wording) and switch it **OFF**.
</Step>
<Step>
**Confirm Your Choice**

You may be asked to confirm. Click **"Not now"** or similar to confirm you're opting out.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When You Turn It Off?

- Your new conversations will **not** be used to train Claude
- Your data is kept for only 30 days (instead of 5 years if opted in)
- Your chat history is still saved for your convenience
- The setting applies to all your future conversations

## Important Dates

<Callout type="info">
If you're an existing Claude user, you have until **October 8, 2025** to make your choice about data training. After that date, you'll need to select your preference to continue using Claude.
</Callout>

## What About Previous Chats?

- If you've been using Claude before opting out, some previous conversations may have already been included in training
- Once you opt out, **new chats** will not be used
- Deleting a conversation removes it from future training use

## Mobile App Settings

If you use Claude on your phone:

<Steps>
<Step>
Tap the menu icon (usually top-left)
</Step>
<Step>
Tap your profile at the bottom
</Step>
<Step>
Go to **Privacy**
</Step>
<Step>
Turn off **"You can help improve Claude"**
</Step>
<Step>
Tap **"Not now"** to confirm
</Step>
</Steps>

## Summary

| Setting | What to do |
|---------|------------|
| Help improve Claude | Turn **OFF** |
| Data retention when opted out | 30 days |
| Data retention when opted in | 5 years |

<Callout type="info">
This setting only applies to personal Claude accounts (Free, Pro, Max). Business and Enterprise accounts have different, stricter privacy protections by default.
</Callout>
