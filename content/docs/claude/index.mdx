# What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant made by a company called Anthropic. Like ChatGPT, you can ask it questions and get helpful responses. Claude is especially good at working with longer documents and having thoughtful conversations.

## What Can Claude Do?

Claude can help with many everyday tasks:

- **Write and edit** - Emails, reports, documents, and more
- **Analyze documents** - Upload files and ask questions about them
- **Research topics** - Get thorough explanations on any subject
- **Brainstorm ideas** - Generate creative ideas and solutions
- **Summarize content** - Turn long texts into clear summaries
- **Answer questions** - Get detailed, thoughtful answers

## What Makes Claude Different?

- **Longer conversations** - Claude can handle very long documents (up to 500 pages worth!)
- **Projects feature** - Organize your work and keep context across multiple chats
- **File uploads** - Upload PDFs, Word docs, and more for Claude to analyze

## Is It Free?

**Yes!** Claude has a free version that's great for getting started.

There are paid plans (Pro, Max) with more features, but the free version works well for most people.
