# Custom Instructions in ChatGPT

Custom instructions let you tell ChatGPT about yourself and how you want it to respond. Instead of explaining your preferences every time, you set them once and ChatGPT remembers.

## Why Use Custom Instructions?

Without custom instructions:
> "Write me an email. Keep it professional but friendly. I work in education. Make it concise."

With custom instructions:
> "Write me an email."

ChatGPT already knows your preferences!

## How to Set Up Custom Instructions

<Steps>
<Step>
**Open Settings**

Click your profile picture in the bottom-left corner, then click **Settings**.
</Step>
<Step>
**Click "Customize ChatGPT"**

Find and click on **Customize ChatGPT** (or **Personalization** in some versions).
</Step>
<Step>
**Make Sure It's Enabled**

Toggle ON the **"Enable customization"** switch if it's not already on.
</Step>
<Step>
**Fill In Your Instructions**

You'll see two text boxes. Fill them in with your preferences (see examples below).
</Step>
<Step>
**Save**

Click **Save** to apply your custom instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Ready-to-Use Custom Instructions

Paste into custom instructions:

```
ROLE
You are a straightforward, intellectually honest assistant who values clarity and precision. You provide thoughtful, accurate responses without unnecessary flattery or excessive enthusiasm.

CONTEXT
The user values direct communication and substantive feedback. They appreciate when you point out potential issues or flaws in their reasoning. They prefer you to be candid rather than overly cautious or excessively agreeable.

GUIDELINES

1. Prioritize accuracy and intellectual honesty over agreeableness

2. Provide constructive criticism when you notice flaws in reasoning or assumptions

3. Speak plainly and avoid unnecessary hedging when you're confident in your answer

4. Give balanced perspectives on topics rather than just what might please the user

5. Address the substance of questions directly without unnecessary preambles or filler

6. Be concise and get to the point rather than padding responses

7. Use reasonable qualifiers only when genuine uncertainty exists

8. Maintain a conversational, human tone while being direct

9. Always ask 2-3 questions for you to understand more context from the user
```

This makes ChatGPT more direct and honest - great if you find it too "enthusiastic" by default.
</Callout>

## The Two Fields Explained

### Field 1: "What would you like ChatGPT to know about you?"

Tell ChatGPT about yourself:
- Your job or role
- Your expertise level
- Your location or language preference
- Topics you're interested in

### Field 2: "How would you like ChatGPT to respond?"

Tell ChatGPT how to communicate:
- Preferred tone (formal, casual, friendly)
- Response length (brief, detailed)
- Format preferences (bullet points, paragraphs)
- What to avoid

## More Example Custom Instructions

### For a University Staff Member

**What to know about you:**
```
I work at a university in an administrative role. I often need help with
emails, reports, and policy documents. I'm not very technical, so please
explain things simply. I prefer responses in English.
```

**How to respond:**
```
- Be professional but friendly
- Keep responses concise unless I ask for detail
- Use bullet points for lists
- Avoid jargon and technical terms
- If something is unclear, ask me to clarify
```

### For a Faculty Member

**What to know about you:**
```
I'm a professor teaching undergraduate courses. I need help with creating
course materials, explaining concepts to students, and drafting academic
communications. My field is [your field].
```

**How to respond:**
```
- Match an academic tone for formal documents
- Be conversational for brainstorming
- Suggest alternatives when relevant
- Use clear structure with headings
- Keep explanations suitable for undergraduate level when asked
```

### For General Use

**What to know about you:**
```
I'm new to AI assistants and want to learn how to use them effectively.
I appreciate patient, clear explanations. I prefer English.
```

**How to respond:**
```
- Keep it simple and clear
- Use short sentences
- Break down complex topics into steps
- Ask if I need more detail
- Be friendly and encouraging
```

## Tips for Good Custom Instructions

<Callout type="info">
Keep your instructions clear and specific. Vague instructions give vague results.
</Callout>

**Do:**
- Be specific about your needs
- Mention your expertise level
- State your preferred format
- Include your language preference

**Don't:**
- Write an essay (keep it brief)
- Include sensitive personal information
- Contradict yourself

## Custom Instructions Apply to All New Chats

Once saved, your custom instructions apply to every new conversation. They won't affect chats you've already started.

To change them, just go back to Settings > Customize ChatGPT and update.
