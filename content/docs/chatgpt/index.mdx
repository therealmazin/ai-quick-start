# What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is like having a smart assistant on your computer. You type a question or request, and it responds with helpful answers. It's made by a company called OpenAI.

## What Can ChatGPT Do?

Here are some everyday things ChatGPT can help with:

- **Write emails** - Draft professional emails in seconds
- **Explain things** - Get simple explanations of complex topics
- **Summarize documents** - Turn long texts into short summaries
- **Brainstorm ideas** - Generate ideas for projects, presentations, or events
- **Answer questions** - Get answers to almost any question
- **Help with writing** - Improve your writing, fix grammar, or change the tone

![ChatGPT in action](/images/chatgpt_intro.gif)

## Is It Free?

**Yes!** ChatGPT has a free version that works great for most people.

There are paid plans (Plus, Pro) with extra features, but you don't need them to get started.
